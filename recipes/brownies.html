3 Ingredient Hazelnut Brownies

Hazelnut Brownies

This recipe is a very easy to make Hazelnut brownies. It only uses 3 ingredients!

    Ingredients 8 servings
  • 13 oz chocolate hazelnut spread (370 g)
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup flour (65 g)
    Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF (175ºC).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ingredients.
  3. Transfer mix to square baking dish.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool for 5 minutes and then carefully cut into squares.
  6. Enjoy!
    Nutrition Info per serving
  • Calories 300
  • Fat 15g
  • Carbs 35g
  • Fiber 2g
  • Sugar 24g
  • Protein 5g
